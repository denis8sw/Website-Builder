What is DeisgnToCode?

DesignToCode is a website builder that simplifies the coding process by generating code automatically from your designs. With DesignToCode, you can transform your website designs into functional code in just a few clicks, without having to write a single line of code yourself.

Can I create a responsive website with your builder?

Our website builder does not currently offer a range of options for creating a responsive website. However, it is still possible to create a responsive design manually, as we do not place any limitations on the way you position your website elements.

How do I deploy my website to the internet?

Once you have generated the code from your design using DesignToCode, deploying your website to the internet is a simple process. All you need to do is copy the generated code and upload it to a hosting service, which can be either free or paid.

How secure is your platform?

DesignToCode provides a secure environment for users to design and publish their websites without the need to save their work on our servers. Our platform operates entirely within the user's browser, with all data saved locally on their device.

Is there a limit to the number of pages I can create on my website?

We apologize for any confusion. Currently, our platform does not support the creation of multiple pages for your website. However, our team is constantly working to improve our services and we hope to offer this feature in the future.

How much control do I have over the design and layout of my website?

At DesignToCode, we understand that having control over the design and layout of your website is essential. Our platform provides users with the ability to customize every aspect of their website, from events and positioning to the code generated from their design.
